Chapter 14

  • Intensely praised by Mr. Collins.
  • "He had never in his life witnessed such behaviour in a person of rank- such affability and condescension," from Mr. Collins.
  • She was thought of as proud but Mr. Collins only saw friendliness in her.
  • She spoke to him like she spoke to everyone else, urged him to marry sooner, and approved all of his ideas.
  • Lives in Rosings Park.
  • She is a widow with a daughter who is the heiress of Rosings park.
  • "She is a most charming young lady indeed."
  • She's weak which prevents her from accomplishing many more things.
